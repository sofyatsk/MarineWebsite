Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains how WJM Marine collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, shipping details, and payment information when you create an account or place an order.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process orders, improve our services, and provide customer support.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your information. We only share data with trusted partners necessary for order fulfillment and secure payment processing.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website performance.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, updates, or deletion of your personal information at any time.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@wmjmarine.com
